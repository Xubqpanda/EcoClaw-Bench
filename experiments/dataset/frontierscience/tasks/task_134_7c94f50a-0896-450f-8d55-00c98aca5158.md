## Prompt

Context: Genexol-PM is a polymeric micellar system of paclitaxel. The original procedure states "Briefly, paclitaxel (30 mg) and mPEG-PDLLA (150 mg) were dissolved in 2.0 mL of acetonitrile. After 5 min of stirring, the organic solvent was evaporated on a rotary evaporator under reduced pressure at 60 °C to obtain a transparent gel matrix. The resulting transparent gel matrix was dissolved by the addition of water at 60 °C to obtain a transparent paclitaxel incorporated micellar solution. The solution, obtained in this process, was filtered through a 0.22 um filter and lyophilized by a freeze dryer system (Labconco, USA), to obtain a dried paclitaxel (Genexol (R))-containing biodegradable polymeric micellar system (Genexol-PM)."
Question: Answer the following questions and explain: 

What would be the consequence of stirring for 1 minute instead of 5 minutes? 

What would be the consequence of adding saline at 60 °C instead of water? 

What would be the consequence of adding 5% sucrose at 60 °C instead of water? 

How many peaks are present when viewing the size distribution by intensity using dynamic light scattering (DLS) prior to filtration and what are the hydrodynamic volumes? 

Are there any important steps missing from the published protocol, and if so, what are they? Please identify and explain all missing steps.
