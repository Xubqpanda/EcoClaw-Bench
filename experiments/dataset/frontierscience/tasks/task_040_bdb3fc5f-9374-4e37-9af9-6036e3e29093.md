## Prompt

`\(\vec{E}_i(x,t) = E_0 \cos\left( \omega (t - \frac{x}{c}) \right) \hat{y}\)` where `\(\omega\)` is the angular frequency of the incident light, and `\(c\)` is the speed of light. The vacuum permeability is `\(\mu_0\)`.
